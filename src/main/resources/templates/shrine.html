The following are the current perks available in the Shrine of Secrets:

The Shrine of Secrets resets every Wednesday at 00:00:00 UTC (GMT+0)

Hex: Devour Hope

A hex rooting its power on hope. The false hope of Survivors ignites your hunger. When a Survivor is rescued from a hook at least 24 metres away, Devour Hope receives a token. 2 Tokens: Gain a 3 / 4 / 5 % Haste Status Effect for 10 seconds after hooking a Survivor. 3 Tokens: Survivors suffer from the Exposed Status Effect. 4 Tokens: You are granted the ability to kill Survivors by your own hand.

Ace in the Hole

Lady Luck always seems to be throwing something good your way. Anytime you loot an Item from a Chest, there is a 50 / 75 / 100 % chance that an Add-on of Very Rare Rarity or lower will also be attached to it.

Soul Guard

You have been through immense hardship, and you're stronger for it. After performing a Safe Hook Rescue on another Survivor, the Perk activates. You now have the Broken Status Effect. While this Perk is active, any time you heal a Survivor from the Dying State to Injured, or the Injured State to Healthy, the healed Survivor gains the Endurance Status Effect for a duration of 4 seconds.
